## Grammar and Spelling

I can help correct grammar and spelling. Common fixes: "your" vs "you're", "its" vs "it's", "there" vs "their" vs "they're". A sentence should have a subject and a verb. Use active voice for clearer writing. Capitalize the first word of a sentence and proper nouns. End statements with a period, questions with a question mark. Run-on sentences should be split or joined with a conjunction. Subject-verb agreement matters: "he goes", "they go".
