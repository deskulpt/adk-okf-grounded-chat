# Shipping & Tracking FAQ

**When will my order ship?**
In-stock items dispatch within 24h. Made-to-order ships in 5–7 business days.

**How do I track my package?**
Use the tracking link in your dispatch email. Carriers: Delhivery, BlueDart, Ecom.

**My tracking isn't updating.**
Carrier scans lag up to 24h after pickup. If no scan in 3 days, open a ticket.

**Do you ship internationally?**
Yes — currently to US, UK, UAE, SG. Duties calculated at checkout.
